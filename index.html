About! my school!

It is a Government School and is part of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) and is managed by Public Sector Undertaking. Kendriya Vidyalaya KanchanBagh, Hyderabad is one of the best Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) schools in Kanchan Bagh, Hyderabad and ranks among the top schools in Hyderabad.

What's New

Online Admission are open for STD I from Jul 20, 2020 for the Academic Year 2020-21. Higher Class Procedure:Admission is a matter of availability of seats in the desired class. The Students seeking admission to the school must apply on a prescribed form, available at the School Reception.
